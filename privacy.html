Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2024

Introduction

Welcome to Lush Craft. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you visit our educational website. As an educational project affiliated with Edith Cowan University (ECU), we are committed to maintaining the privacy and security of your information.

Educational Purpose

This website is an educational project created as part of the GDI2100.1 Web Design course at Edith Cowan University. The primary purpose is to demonstrate web design principles, sustainable practices documentation, and botanical craft education.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

As an educational demonstration website, we do not actively collect personal information from visitors. However, standard web server logs may automatically record:

  • IP addresses
  • Browser type and version
  • Operating system
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website addresses
  • Date and time of access

Cookies

This website may use cookies to enhance user experience. Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us:

  • Remember your preferences
  • Understand how you use the website
  • Improve website functionality

You can control cookie settings through your browser preferences.

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely for:

  • Educational analysis and demonstration purposes
  • Improving website functionality and user experience
  • Understanding visitor patterns for academic research
  • Maintaining website security
  • Course assessment and evaluation

Educational Partner - ECU

This project is created in partnership with Edith Cowan University. Any data collected may be used for:

  • Academic assessment purposes
  • Educational research within the course scope
  • Demonstration of web design principles

For more information about ECU's privacy policies, please visit: ECU Privacy Statement

Third-Party Services

This website may use third-party services including:

Google Fonts

We use Google Fonts to display typography. Google may collect usage data. Review Google's Privacy Policy for details.

External Links

Our website contains links to external sites, including:

  • Edith Cowan University (ECU) website
  • Social media platforms (Instagram, Facebook, Pinterest)

We are not responsible for the privacy practices of external websites. Please review their respective privacy policies.

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of information. However, as an educational project, please note:

  • This is not a commercial website collecting sensitive data
  • We do not process payments or financial information
  • We do not collect personally identifiable information through forms
  • Standard web hosting security protocols apply

Children's Privacy

This website is designed for educational purposes and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided information, please contact us.

Your Rights

As a visitor to this educational website, you have the right to:

  • Access information about how your data is used
  • Request information we may hold about you
  • Disable cookies through your browser settings
  • Stop using the website at any time

International Users

This website is hosted in Australia and subject to Australian privacy laws. By accessing this site from outside Australia, you consent to the transfer and processing of your information in accordance with Australian law.

Changes to This Policy

As an educational project, this privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in:

  • Website functionality
  • Educational requirements
  • Privacy regulations
  • Technical implementation

The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page indicates when changes were made. Continued use of the website after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

No Commercial Activity

This is an educational demonstration website. We do not:

  • Sell or trade products
  • Process payments or transactions
  • Collect credit card information
  • Ship physical products
  • Maintain customer accounts

All product listings are for educational and demonstrative purposes only.

Sustainable Practices & Transparency

In line with our commitment to transparency and sustainable practices, we maintain radical transparency about:

  • How this educational website operates
  • The academic context of this project
  • Our partnership with ECU
  • The demonstrative nature of our content
